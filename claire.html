BackStory

Clarinet—Claire’s mother was infatuated with the instrument, and because of that, she was given the name. Claire was born and grew up in a middle-class family a few miles from Berlin, Germany. When she was mid-twenties, she fell in love with an American soldier by the name of Patrick Preston. Despite her parents’ best wishes, she sought to start her life with him in America.

At first, everything was great. Claire secured a job, and Patrick was making money as a security guard at a few high-profile clubs after retiring from the military. A few months later, they decided to adopt an eleven-year-old boy by the name of Michael shortly after Claire was deemed infertile by her physician.

As the years went on, however, Patrick got into deep with the sharks as his gambling addiction began to consume both his mind and body. Upon his passing, Claire was left alone with no job and a hungry and a quite dependent son Michael.

Affiliations

Claire is a member of the custodial force located in Springfield High School. She also has an independent business cleaning homes of wealthy people on the weekends. As a side note, she has a three-year expired membership to Retro Fitness (who has the time, right).

When it comes to family, she belongs to the Preston lineage along with its final air, Michael Preston.
